Help & FAQ
Everything you need to know about using the platform
How do I become a vendor?
Register for an account and select 'Vendor / Business'. Once submitted, our admin team will review your application and verify your business.
How does delivery work?
Most vendors offer delivery directly to your hall of residence or a central campus pickup point. Specifics are handled during the order process.
What payment methods are accepted?
We support Mobile Money, Cash on Delivery, and Card payments depending on the vendor's preference.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we prioritize student privacy. Your data is stored locally and used only for order processing.
